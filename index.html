Flavors That Dance In Your Mouth

Quality Cuisine and High End-Food

Nestled in Nairobi's Kilimani area, The Nouveau Table exudes vintage charm, inviting patrons to a culinary journey back in time. This quaint restaurant prides itself on serving farm-fresh ingredients and scratch-made delicacies, reminiscent of a bygone era. Stepping through its doors, guests are greeted with the aroma of wholesome cooking and the warmth of rustic décor. From hearty soups to artisanal bread, each dish celebrates simplicity and quality. The Nouveau Table is more than just a restaurant; it's a welcoming haven where guests can enjoy delicious meals made from scratch in a cozy setting that pays homage to the timeless art of cooking.

  • Breakfast

    Indulge in the morning with our Breakfast Special, featuring fluffy pancakes served with locally sourced honey and a side of seasonal fruits. For a heartier option, try our Farmhouse Omelette, filled with fresh vegetables and topped with melted cheese. Pair your meal with a steaming cup of freshly brewed coffee or a revitalizing fruit smoothie to kickstart your day.

  • Lunch

    Our Lunch menu offers a delightful array of options to satisfy your midday cravings. Enjoy our Grilled Chicken Caesar Salad, made with crisp lettuce, juicy grilled chicken breast, parmesan cheese, and homemade Caesar dressing. For something more substantial, savor our Classic Beef Burger, served with hand-cut fries and a tangy house-made dipping sauce. Don't forget to explore our daily soup specials for a comforting bowl of warmth.

  • Dinner

    As the sun sets, treat yourself to a memorable dining experience with our Dinner selections. Begin your evening with our Chef's Special Appetizer Platter, featuring a medley of artisanal cheeses, cured meats, and house-made dips. For the main course, indulge in our Pan-Seared Salmon, perfectly seasoned and served with a side of roasted vegetables and herb-infused quinoa. Finish your meal on a sweet note with our decadent Chocolate Lava Cake, accompanied by a scoop of creamy vanilla ice cream.

Indulge your sweet tooth and savor the perfect ending to your dining experience at The Nouveau Table.

Whether you're in the mood for something light and refreshing, bold and adventurous, or sweet and indulgent, our menu offers something for every palate. Join us and embark on a journey of flavor and discovery as you sip your way through our carefully crafted cocktail creations.

From crisp and refreshing whites to rich and velvety reds, each wine on our menu is selected for its exceptional quality, distinctive character, and ability to complement our culinary offerings. Whether you're a seasoned oenophile or a casual wine enthusiast, our menu offers something to satisfy every palate and occasion.
